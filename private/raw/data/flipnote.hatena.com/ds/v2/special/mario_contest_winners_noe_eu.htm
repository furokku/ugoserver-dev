Make a Mario Flipnote
We would like to thank everybody who contributed their fantastic Flipnotes, which were a perfect way to help us celebrate the 25th Anniversary of Super Mario Bros. Here are just some of the Flipnotes we picked, with comments from the creators of Super Mario Bros. (Shigeru Miyamoto, Takashi Tezuka, and Yoshiaki Koizumi). These Flipnotes can also be viewed online at the Mario Anniversary website, and on the Nintendo Channel on your Wii console too! Thank you for your participation!
We are featuring 8 Flipnotes, which you can view below!
Creator: Raving Boy
